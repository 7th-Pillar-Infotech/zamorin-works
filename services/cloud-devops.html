Cloud and DevOps that ship without surprises.

Migrations to AWS, Azure, or GCP. Infrastructure-as-code. Delivery pipelines that move fast without breaking production. The discipline that lets engineering teams stop worrying about the floor and start building on it.

Code · CLD

What's included

  • Cloud migration. Lift-and-shift, re-platform, or re-architect — chosen by what your workloads actually need.
  • Infrastructure-as-code. Terraform, Pulumi, or CloudFormation. Reproducible, versioned, reviewed like application code.
  • CI/CD pipelines. GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, Azure DevOps — pipelines that fail loudly and recover quietly.
  • Container & orchestration. Docker, Kubernetes, ECS, Cloud Run — picked against your team's operational capacity.
  • Observability. Logs, metrics, traces, alerts that wake the right person and only the right person.
  • Cost engineering. Right-sizing, reserved instances, savings plans — usually 20–40% reductions on first pass.
Fit · CLD

Right for these situations

You're moving from on-prem to cloud, your AWS bill has crept beyond reason, deployments are manual and scary, or you've got a Kubernetes cluster nobody fully understands. You want to fix it without lifting and re-shifting.

Less ideal: you want to move all workloads to cloud as fast as possible without re-architecting. We'll do it — but we'll tell you upfront which parts will hurt later.

Shape · CLD

Typical engagement

6 to 24 weeks for migrations; ongoing for embedded DevOps work. Discover phase fixed-fee. We work alongside your engineering team, not around it.
