A senior engineering bench, on retainer.

Ongoing support, monitoring, and second-line engineering capacity for in-house teams. The bench you wish you had — without the hiring overhead, without the long-term commitment of a fixed payroll line.

Code · MIT

What's included

  • Application support. Tier-2 and tier-3 engineering support for systems we built or inherited.
  • Infrastructure operations. Cloud platform monitoring, patching, scaling, on-call coverage.
  • Incremental enhancements. Feature work and tech-debt cleanup between major projects, billed against a monthly capacity pool.
  • Security operations. Continuous monitoring, alert triage, incident response in partnership with your security team.
  • Vendor coordination. Single throat to choke when multiple vendors are involved — we run the meetings, you don't.
  • Quarterly health reviews. Written review of what we did, what broke, what's coming, what we'd change.
Fit · MIT

Right for these situations

You have a small IT or engineering team carrying a system bigger than their capacity. You need senior engineering availability without hiring senior engineers full-time. Or you've finished a major build and want the team that built it to keep supporting it.

Less ideal: you need fully outsourced first-line helpdesk. We're not a traditional MSP — we're a senior engineering bench you can rent.

Shape · MIT

Typical engagement

3-month minimum, usually 12 months rolling. Monthly capacity pool with named lead. Quarterly review to right-size the commitment.
