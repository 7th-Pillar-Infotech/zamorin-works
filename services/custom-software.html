Code · SFT

What's included

  • Discovery & product design. User research, journey mapping, wireframes, and a written specification before any code.
  • Architecture & tech selection. Boring tools when they fit, novel ones only when they pay for themselves.
  • Web applications. React, Next.js, Node, .NET — chosen against your team's maintenance capacity.
  • Mobile applications. Native (Swift, Kotlin) or cross-platform — picked for the right reasons.
  • API & integration work. REST/GraphQL services, third-party integrations, clean contracts between systems.
  • QA, deployment & handover. Automated tests, CI/CD, documentation your team can actually read.
Fit · SFT

Right for these situations

You've outgrown spreadsheets, off-the-shelf SaaS doesn't fit your process, or your existing custom app has become unmaintainable. You want a partner who'll think with you about what to build — not just an order-taker.

Less ideal: you need 10 developers tomorrow and have already scoped exactly what they'll build. We sell engagements, not seat-hours.
