A defensible plan, not another deck.

Digital roadmaps, technology assessments, and vendor selection — grounded in your commercial reality, not the latest analyst quadrant. You walk away with a plan you can actually fund and execute.

Code · STR

What's included

  • Current-state assessment. A written read on where your technology actually is — not where the previous consultant said it was.
  • Digital roadmap. Sequenced initiatives with dependencies, owners, and budget bands. Defensible at the board level.
  • Technology evaluation. Vendor or platform shortlists with weighted criteria you set, not us.
  • Build-vs-buy analysis. Honest cost-of-ownership comparison over a 3-year horizon, including the parts vendors leave out.
  • Org & sourcing recommendations. What to build in-house, what to outsource, what to keep on retainer.
  • Executive briefing pack. Slides you can present to your board without editing.
Fit · STR

Right for these situations

You're a mid-to-large enterprise in Dubai or the GCC that has accumulated technology debt and wants a clear-headed plan to move forward. Or you're entering a new market and need a sourcing strategy for IT before you commit to vendors.

Less ideal: you're looking for a brand-name consultancy to validate a decision that's already been made. We'd rather decline than rubber-stamp.

Shape · STR

Typical engagement

4 to 10 weeks. Fixed fee. One Zamorin Works principal co-leads with one or two specialists. Final deliverable always includes a 30-minute briefing for your executive team.
