§ Working together
What this means

The principals you meet are the principals who deliver.

Many consultancies use senior partners to win the work and junior consultants to do it. We don't. Every engagement is co-led by Vikram or Pallavi directly — they attend kick-off, weekly status reviews, and final handover.

This is only sustainable because we don't take on more work than we can personally steward. If we're too busy to give your engagement the attention it deserves, we'll tell you upfront.
