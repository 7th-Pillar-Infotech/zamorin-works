§ Standards
Operating discipline

Four standards we hold across every engagement.

The discipline a client experiences is the same one our founders insist on — week after week, regardless of scope.

01

Single accountable partner.

One Dubai-based principal owns each engagement, end to end — sales, scope, delivery, outcomes.

02

Written architecture before code.

Every build begins with an agreed architecture document — diagrams, ADRs, choices and refusals — that both sides sign off on.

03

Predictability over heroics.

Unscheduled overtime is a planning failure. Schedules are negotiated, not promised; renegotiated, not abandoned.

04

Refuse the wrong work.

A firm is measured by what it declines. We turn down work outside our discipline, even when it is well-paid.

A working principle
We treat unscheduled overtime as a planning failure, not a virtue. Discipline beats heroics — quietly, every week, for the length of the engagement. — An operating belief, inherited from twenty years of Zamorin Group delivery
