§ What happens next
The first call

The first conversation is always free.

We don't charge for scoping calls. We don't bill for "discovery workshops" before you've decided to work with us. The first thirty minutes are an honest conversation about whether we're the right fit — and a straight answer if we're not.

If we're the right fit, we propose a fixed-fee Discover phase as the next step. If we're not, we point you to someone who is.
