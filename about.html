A consultancy built on twenty years of operating discipline.

Zamorin Works is the technology arm of the Zamorin Group — a Dubai- and Kochi-rooted business known for refined craftsmanship and PMI-certified project delivery. We've taken that same discipline and applied it to enterprise technology.

§ Why we exist
The thesis

Most IT engagements fail on coordination, not code.

Talk to any operations leader in Dubai and you'll hear the same complaints: too many vendors, no single point of accountability, scope that drifts, status reports that obscure rather than inform.

Zamorin Works exists to fix that. One accountable principal in Dubai owns each engagement — scope, delivery, and outcomes — without subcontracting responsibility.

§ Standards
What we believe

Four tenets that guide every engagement.

Inherited from the Zamorin Group's operating culture. Written down so we can be held to them.

01

Discipline over heroics.

Predictable rhythm beats last-minute scrambles. We plan in writing, communicate weekly, and treat unscheduled overtime as a planning failure, not a virtue.

02

Clarity over cleverness.

The best technical solution is the one a smaller team can maintain in three years. Boring tools when they fit; novel ones only when they pay for themselves.

03

One owner per engagement.

Not a "team." A named principal with their signature on the timeline. You have their direct number.

04

Outcomes, then output.

Shipping features is not success. We define what changes for the business — and how we'll measure it — before any line of code is written.
