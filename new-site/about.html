Our story

We built this because we needed it too.

Born out of the reality of running multiple businesses simultaneously — discovering that most companies don't have the right tools, systems, or support to grow efficiently. We built the solution we wished existed.

Running a business is exciting. It's also relentless.

Between managing operations, navigating technology decisions, and keeping back-office functions from falling apart, most SME founders spend more time in the business than on it.

Zamorin Works exists to change that. We are a firm specialising in AI automation, IT consulting, and business support for SMEs and startups wherever they operate.

Part of the Zamorin Group of Companies — built on the belief that smart systems and clear thinking unlock extraordinary results.
